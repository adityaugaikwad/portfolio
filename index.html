About Me

Creating Intelligent Embedded Solutions

I am an Embedded System Engineer with strong expertise in system design, firmware development, and hardware-software integration. My professional experience includes working on advanced robotics, autonomous navigation, and electric vehicle systems.

I am passionate about building intelligent embedded solutions that combine real-time control with modern communication protocols to solve real-world challenges. I have hands-on experience in developing and optimizing embedded applications using C, C++, and RTOS, as well as working with microcontrollers, sensors, and actuators.

My work spans across domains such as IoT, AI-driven embedded vision, and automotive systems. I aim to push the boundaries of embedded technology to create reliable, efficient, and scalable products.
